— How it works

Four steps, streamed live.

From upload to a categorized, auditable redacted PDF — watched in real time as it happens.

1
Ingest

Ingest the PDF

Your document is read in your environment — page text, layout and coordinates parsed for analysis. Nothing leaves your control.

2
Sweep

Regex sweeps every page

Deterministic patterns catch emails, SSNs, phone and account numbers for true redaction — text is removed, not masked.

3
Classify

14 concurrent Gemma-4 chunks

The document is chunked and classified in parallel on Cerebras, flagging names, addresses and IDs that regex alone would miss.

4
Deliver

Categorized, auditable PDF

A redacted PDF with a categorized audit trail of every item removed — streamed back live as redaction completes.

— Built for the enterprise

Privacy you can prove.

Redactly is designed for teams that handle confidential documents and cannot afford a leak.
